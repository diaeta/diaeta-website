It is this methodical approach that allows us to offer lasting results where others fail.

The Founding Pillars of the Diaeta Method

{# Use style from Poids page, adapted #}

1. In-depth Analysis and Precise Diagnosis

Everything starts with understanding you. Through a detailed questionnaire, an open discussion about your lifestyle, habits, medical history, and a precise body composition analysis (fat mass, muscle, water...), we establish your unique metabolic and nutritional profile. This comprehensive understanding is the essential first step to designing a strategy that perfectly aligns with your tastes and real needs, thus ensuring a positive experience and results without unnecessary frustration.

2. A Personalized Strategy, Designed for Your Life

Based on the initial analysis and science, we design YOUR plan. It's not about prohibitions, but about smart strategies for satiety, pleasure, and integration into your daily life (social meals included!). Your preferences are at the heart of the plan, so that eating remains a source of satisfaction and not a constraint, even while achieving your health goals.

3. Dynamic Follow-up & Connected Tools

Thanks to our mobile application and regular follow-up consultations (with body composition analysis), we track your progress in real time. This allows for quick and precise adjustments to optimize results and maintain your motivation. This interactive follow-up and proactive adjustments ensure that your nutritional journey remains not only effective but also enjoyable and adapted to your life, thus avoiding any unnecessary frustration and ensuring you continue to enjoy your meals.

4. Expert Guidance & Autonomy

Your dietitian is a coach and an educator. We explain the 'why' behind each recommendation, give you the tools to make informed choices, and support you in becoming autonomous in managing your health. This autonomy, based on a solid understanding and expert guidance, is the key to sustainably maintaining a diet that is a source of pleasure and well-being, far from the constraints of short-lived diets.

The Diaeta Method in Action: A Concrete Example

{# Optimized image for app communication #}

Imagine this common scenario:

  1. You are anticipating a restaurant meal or a busy weekend and wonder how to best manage it.
  2. You send a simple and quick message to your dietitian via the secure mobile app.
  3. Your dietitian reviews your current plan, your recent progress, and replies with concrete, personalized advice (e.g., dish choices, adjustments before/after).
  4. During your next follow-up, the body composition analysis confirms that you have stayed on track despite the unexpected events.
  5. We analyze together what worked and strengthen your confidence to handle similar situations in the future.

That's the Diaeta Method: proactive, connected, and personalized support that truly adapts to your life for lasting results.

Another example (IBS Management): During the elimination phase of the Low FODMAP diet, a patient logs their meals via the app. Their Diaeta dietitian reviews this log daily, quickly identifies a hidden source of FODMAPs in a dish, and immediately alerts them, while suggesting a tasty alternative. This direct exchange allows the patient to learn quickly, avoid discomfort, and continue the phase with confidence and success.
