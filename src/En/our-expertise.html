About Pierre Abou-Zeid

Experienced Dietitian-Nutritionist

With my experience and a passion for the science of nutrition, I am committed to providing you with the tools and knowledge necessary to regain control of your health. My background, including a Bachelor's degree in Dietetics from Erasmus University College and previous training as a Programmer and IT Analyst, gives me a unique perspective, combining scientific rigor with an analytical approach to solve your nutritional challenges.

As a Dietitian-Nutritionist certified by INAMI and certified by MONASH University for the Low FODMAP approach in managing Irritable Bowel Syndrome, I am continuously at the forefront of the latest advancements to offer you the best care.

I am also a member of the UPDLF (Professional Union of French-Speaking Dietitians), a testament to my commitment to the highest professional standards.

My Philosophy & Approach

Scientific Foundations & Advanced Personalization

My approach is based on deep scientific expertise and the most recent evidence-based data, translated into highly personalized nutritional strategies. Each plan is meticulously adapted to your unique metabolism, your lifestyle, and your individual preferences.

Lasting Results Without Frustration

The goal is to achieve sustainable health improvements without the frustrations of restrictive diets. I firmly believe that a healthy diet should be a source of pleasure and energy, not deprivation. My patients discover how to eat delicious foods while reaching their goals, without ever feeling hungry.

Patient Empowerment & Education

I guide my patients to become informed actors in their own health, providing them with the knowledge and tools necessary to understand their bodies and make confident, autonomous food choices.

Innovation & Continuous Improvement

Passionate about advances in nutrition, I am committed to continuous learning to integrate the most innovative and effective strategies, including the perspectives offered by nutrigenomics.

Commitment to Patient Satisfaction

I am dedicated to offering caring support and solutions that work, as evidenced by the consistent positive feedback from my patients. Your success and well-being are my priority.

Holistic Vision (centered on nutrition)

Although my expertise is centered on dietetics, I recognize the importance of a global view of health, where nutrition interacts with physical activity and mental well-being for optimal results.

My Specific Areas of Expertise

  • Personalized nutrition for adults (18-88 years)
  • Sustainable weight loss and weight management
  • Nutritional management of type 2 diabetes
  • Lipid profile optimization (cholesterol and hypertension management)
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) and the Low FODMAP approach (MONASH University Certified)
  • Introduction to Nutrigenomics (exploring the genetic influence on nutrition)
  • Dietary strategies for overall metabolic health
